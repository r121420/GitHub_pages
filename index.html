MESSAGEメッセージ

Hello! This is the ESS Club of Kobe City College of Technology.
We are very happy to have this opportunity to interact with you all.

In our club, we want to improve our English skills by sharing multinational cultures.
During this Japanese cooking experience, we will help you so you can cook safely and happily.
If you have any allergies or dietary restrictions, please let us know in advance.
We will try our best to prepare alternative ingredients for you.
We will explain the cooking steps clearly, so please join in even if you have never cooked before.

Let’s have fun together and make delicious Japanese food!
If you have any questions or concerns, please feel free to ask.
We hope to have a great time together!

From　Keito Tachibana, the president of ESS Club
